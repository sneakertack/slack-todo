*Description: Todo functionality provided by Kid Haystakt.*

/todo add <your task>
/todo <your task>
Adds a todo task under your name.

/todo show
/todo list
/todo ls
/todo
Lists outstanding todos. Will show EVERYONE's todos.

/todo clear <id>
/todo x <id>
Clear todo under your name with the corresponding number. Use '/todo' to show IDs.
